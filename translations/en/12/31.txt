(31) So when she heard of their scheming, she sent for them and prepared for them a banquet and gave each one of them a knife and said [to Joseph], "Come out before them." And when they saw him, they greatly admired him and cut their hands⁵⁹³ and said, "Perfect is Allāh!⁵⁹⁴ This is not a man; this is none but a noble angel."
