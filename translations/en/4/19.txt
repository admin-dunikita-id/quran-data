(19) O you who have believed, it is not lawful for you to inherit women by compulsion.¹⁷³ And do not make difficulties for them in order to take [back] part of what you gave them¹⁷⁴ unless they commit a clear immorality [i.e., adultery]. And live with them in kindness. For if you dislike them - perhaps you dislike a thing and Allāh makes therein much good.
