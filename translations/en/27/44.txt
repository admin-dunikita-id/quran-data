(44) She was told, "Enter the palace." But when she saw it, she thought it was a body of water¹⁰⁷⁷ and uncovered her shins [to wade through]. He said, "Indeed, it is a palace [whose floor is] made smooth with glass." She said, "My Lord, indeed I have wronged myself, and I submit with Solomon to Allāh, Lord of the worlds."
