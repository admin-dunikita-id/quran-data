(60) And [remember, O Muḥammad], when We told you, "Indeed, your Lord has encompassed the people."⁷⁵⁷ And We did not make the sight which We showed you⁷⁵⁸ except as a trial for the people, as was the accursed tree [mentioned] in the Qur’ān. And We threaten [i.e., warn] them, but it increases them not except in great transgression.
